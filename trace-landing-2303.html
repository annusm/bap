Misalignment as the hero insight

The gap between what you
claim and what you prove.

Misalignment occurs when different parts of your brand communicate different things. It is often invisible without structured analysis.

Promise-proof gaps
Claiming expertise without demonstrating it through content, design, or customer proof.
ICP confusion
Targeting everyone, resonating with no one messaging that fails to speak to a specific buyer.
Design-message contradictions
Serious copy on a playful visual system, or vice versa. The experience undermines the claim.
Channel incoherence
Different voices across platforms. Each channel seems fine in isolation, but the whole is fractured.
